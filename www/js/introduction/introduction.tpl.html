Welcome to Clicker!

Clicker allows you to quickly count and provides you with all customization and statistics you will need.

The Counter

The heart of this application is the simple to use counter. Just click on every part of your touchscreen to increase the counter.

The current counter is shown in the middle so you always see the amount.

Try out some gestures like swiping or holding the finger on your screen. This will allow you to handle further action.

Customization

Want to change the look or behavior of clicker? No Problem.

The settings view allows you to choose a theme and enable animations and sound. Make clicker your own.

You can also change the action for each gesture which you can use on the counter view. Every gesture can be combined with every action.

Statistics

All the information you will need can be viewed on the statistics page.

Charts with detailed trends of your clicks, information about the click amount per time and various other information.

You can also export these statistics!

Let's go

That is all to know about clicker.

Have fun and start clicking.
